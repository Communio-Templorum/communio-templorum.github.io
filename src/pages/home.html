Bibliotheca

We are the Main Library of the Communio Templorum. We are working to collect important books and make them available for all members to share.

  • Scriptorium: We work to preserve ancient texts documenting the history of Rome, the Religio Romana, and the Roman peoples.
  • Cuneiform Text Corpus: Archive of ancient texts written in cuneiform, the earliest written records and religious stories. Languages include Sumerian and Akkadian.

Romanorum Theological Seminary

Our mission is to teach proper worship of the Religio Romana, for which we provide the following services:

  • Provide help and support for all peoples looking to learn more about the Gods.
  • A course for people looking for formal initiation as a full member of the Communio Templorum Romanorum.
  • A course of study for people looking to be priests of the Religio Romana to perform public rites and find new followers.
